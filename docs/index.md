## Find Care Fast (Popular Categories)

- **Doulas** (birth & postpartum) — where to look, what to ask, how to budget  
- **Lactation (IBCLC/CLC)** — when to call, clinic vs. in-home vs. telehealth  
- **Pelvic Floor Therapy** — recovery after birth, pain, incontinence  
- **Parent Groups & Peer Support** — Black parent meetups and lactation groups

See the full, community-updated provider directory at **[theblknest.org](https://theblknest.org)**.

## How This Works

- **Community-vetted:** Locals submit resources; we review for clarity and usefulness.  
- **Transparent:** Plain-language guides with practical checklists and interview questions.  
- **Living docs:** If something’s missing or outdated, suggest an edit and we’ll update quickly.

Want to help? **Suggest a resource or correction** via our submission flow on **[theblknest.org](https://theblknest.org)**.

## About The BLK Nest

We’re building a trusted, searchable hub so Detroit families don’t have to dig for culturally competent care.  
Your feedback makes these guides better for the next caregiver—thank you for being here.
