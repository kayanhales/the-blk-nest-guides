# Lactation Support

Lactation support is help with feeding — breast, bottle, pumping, or combination feeding.

---

## What Lactation Support Helps With

Lactation consultants can help with:

- Latching issues
- Painful feeding
- Low or oversupply
- Pumping setup and schedules
- Bottle feeding concerns
- Feeding multiples
- Transitioning feeding methods

---

## When to Get Lactation Support

Consider lactation support if:

- Feeding hurts
- Your baby isn’t gaining weight as expected
- You feel stressed or unsure about feeding
- You want to pump but don’t know where to start

---

## How to Find Lactation Support

Start here:

- [International Lactation Consultant Association (IBCLC Finder)](https://ilca.org/why-ibclc/falc/)
- [Black Breastfeeding Week](https://blackbreastfeedingweek.org/)

Search tips:

- Look for IBCLC credentials
- Ask if they offer virtual visits
- Ask if they have experience supporting Black families

---

## Insurance & Cost

- Some insurance plans cover lactation support
- Many hospitals offer initial visits
- Community organizations may offer free or low-cost help
