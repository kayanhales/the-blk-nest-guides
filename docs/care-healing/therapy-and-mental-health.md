# Therapy & Mental Health

Pregnancy and postpartum can affect mental health in serious ways.

---

## Common Reasons Parents Seek Therapy

- Anxiety
- Depression
- Postpartum mood disorders
- Rage or irritability
- Grief or loss
- Birth trauma
- Feeling disconnected or overwhelmed

---

## When to Seek Support

Consider therapy if:

- You feel persistently overwhelmed
- You feel numb or detached
- You’re anxious most of the day
- You’re not sleeping even when you can

If you are thinking about harming yourself or others, seek immediate help.

---

## Where to Find Postpartum Mental Health Support

Start here:

- [Postpartum Support International (PSI)](https://www.postpartum.net/)
- [Therapy for Black Girls](https://therapyforblackgirls.com/)

PSI offers:

- Provider directories
- Support groups
- Helplines
- Resources for Black parents

---

## Insurance & Access

- Many therapists offer virtual sessions
- Some accept insurance; some offer sliding scale
- Ask specifically about postpartum experience
