# Body Care & Healing

Body care after pregnancy is about healing — not “bouncing back.”

---

## Common Postpartum Body Concerns

- Pain or soreness
- Scar healing (C-section or tearing)
- Hormonal skin changes
- Hair loss
- Joint or back pain
- Fatigue and weakness

---

## Types of Body Care That May Help

Depending on your needs, body care may include:

- Pelvic floor therapy
- Physical therapy
- Postpartum massage
- Chiropractic care (with postpartum experience)
- Scar mobilization
- Gentle movement or rehab-based exercise

---

## How to Choose a Provider

When looking for body care support:

- Ask if they specialize in postpartum clients
- Ask about experience with C-sections or birth recovery
- Ask about comfort working with Black patients
- Ask what training they have related to postpartum care

---

## Practical Reminder

Start with:

- One concern
- One provider
- One appointment
