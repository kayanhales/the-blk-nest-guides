# Funding & Benefits for Black Families (Michigan)

This page is a **starting map** to programs Michigan families often use during pregnancy, postpartum, and early childhood. Always confirm details on official sites.

> **Navigation tip:** Start applications on the official Michigan benefits portal. Keep IDs, pay stubs, lease, and birth records ready.

---

## Core Programs to Explore
- **Health coverage (pregnancy & baby)** – Medicaid options for pregnant people and infants/children  
- **Food & nutrition** – WIC and related nutrition support  
- **Cash/basic needs** – cash assistance, utility help, housing support (income/situation dependent)  
- **Child care assistance** – subsidies to reduce daycare/in-home care costs  
- **Early learning** – Head Start/Early Head Start and state preschool  
- **Home visiting** – pregnancy–age 3 family support programs

> Log application dates, case numbers, and contacts.

---

## Tips for a Smoother Application
Create a **document kit**, report changes fast, **appeal** if denied, and request **interpreters/accommodations** if needed.

---

## Community & Bridge Supports
Ask doulas about **community funds/sliding scale**; look for **diaper banks**, **housing/utility aid**, and **ride programs** for appointments.
